About Cambridge Memorial Hospital

Cambridge Memorial Hospital is dedicated to providing exceptional healthcare services. With a team of expert doctors and modern facilities, we ensure that every patient receives the best medical attention.

We offer a wide range of medical specialties, including cardiology, neurology, orthopedics, and pediatrics. Our emergency services are available 24/7 to cater to urgent medical needs.

Our Facilities

CMH provides state-of-the-art facilities including advanced operation theatres, ICU, radiology departments, and specialized care units designed to cater to every medical requirement.
